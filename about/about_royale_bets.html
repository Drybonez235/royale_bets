About Royale Bets

Royale Bets is a thrilling way to engage with Clash Royale streams! Start by choosing the screen name that will be shown on the leaderboard! Then obtain the player tag of the streamer that you are watching. You can locate their streamer tag by going to RoyaleAPI.com and putting in their Clash name. Start making predictions based on how well you think your streamer will do.

Betting Info

Every viewer starts with 5,000 points that can be used to place predictions on live matches.

The default prediction is simple: will the streamer win or lose the battle? But if you're feeling confident, you can toggle additional options:

  • Crowns Taken - Predict how many towers the streamer will destroy
  • Crowns Lost - Predict how many towers they'll lose

How do I start betting?

Simply sign in, watch a streamer, and use your starting 5,000 points to place your first bet. Predictions activate after 5 minutes.

What happens if I toggle crowns on?

If you enable crowns taken or lost, you must guess the exact number to win that part of the bet.

Why is there a 5-minute delay?

This ensures that all bets are fair and not made after results are visible.

How is my payout calculated?

Payouts are based on the streamer's win/loss history and how many prediction toggles you've selected. Riskier bets = higher rewards!

How often does the leaderboard refresh?

Every 2 minutes, along with prediction results.
